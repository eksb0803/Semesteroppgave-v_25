How to Get Here

Our Address

Community Science Center is located at:

123 Scienceveien, 0456 Oslo, Norge

By Train

Take the train to Oslo Central Station (Oslo S). From there, it's a 10-minute taxi ride, or you can transfer to the T-bane or a bus (see below).

By T-bane (Subway)

Take Line 4 or 5 westbound and get off at “Forskningsparken” station. From there it's a 5-minute walk to the museum.

By Bus

Bus lines 37 and 54 stop near the museum. Get off at the “Scienceveien” stop, and walk 2 minutes to the entrance.

By Car

Follow Ring 3 and exit at Blindern. From there, follow signs to “Scienceveien”. There is limited paid parking available on site.

On Foot or by Bicycle

The museum is easily accessible by foot or bicycle from nearby neighborhoods. Bicycle racks are available at the entrance.

Accessibility

Step-free access is available from all public transport options, and the walkways are wheelchair-friendly.
